Parallel (multi-thread) version of the T3D executable is T3Di.1.4.3.exe. It runs much faster that the original version included in ASHTM. This version includes bug fixes and replaces version 1.4.1.

Instructions:
Download T3Di1.4.3.zip, extract the executable, rename it as T3D.exe, and copy over in the installation of ASHTM on your computer.
The executable can be also used as standalone and runs from command prompt.
